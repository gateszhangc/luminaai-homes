FAQ

Common questions about Lumina

Start here if you want to understand what Lumina can do, how it works, and how to get started.

What is Lumina?

Lumina is an all-in-one AI creative platform from BytePlus, powered by the Seedream image generation model and Seedance video generation model. It generates high-quality visual assets including images, videos, and marketing content in one click.

What models power Lumina?

Lumina uses two proprietary ByteDance models: Seedream (image generation, versions 4.5 and 5.0 Lite) and Seedance (video generation, version 1.5). Seedream 5.0 Lite offers deep visual reasoning capabilities.

What can I create with Lumina?

Product displays, character portraits, marketing materials, advertising videos, short-form content, digital human videos, comic-style illustrations, and more. Lumina covers design, marketing, education, and business use cases.

How does credit pricing work?

Lumina uses a credit system. Each image or video generation consumes a number of credits depending on resolution, duration, and model. Credits are shared across all features and models, and unused credits roll over within your billing period.

Can I use the content commercially?

Yes. All generated assets come with watermark-free downloads and commercial usage rights for all plan tiers.

What do Seedream and Seedance mean?

Seedream is ByteDance's image generation model line, and Seedance is its video generation model line. Both are proprietary models developed by ByteDance's AI research teams and are offered through the Lumina platform.
